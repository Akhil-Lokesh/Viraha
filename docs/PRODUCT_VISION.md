# Viraha - Product Vision Document

**Version 1.0**

---

## What is Viraha?

**Viraha** (Sanskrit: विरह) - The ache of separation from what you love.

Viraha is a travel memory collection and discovery platform that helps you preserve the places you've loved and discover new ones through authentic, community-driven content.

## The Problem

Travelers face two key challenges:

### 1. Discovery Fatigue
- **Yelp Overwhelm**: Too many reviews, star ratings feel impersonal
- **Instagram Noise**: Beautiful photos but lacking context and authenticity
- **TripAdvisor Generic**: Corporate, algorithm-driven recommendations
- **Lost Authenticity**: Hard to find genuine local recommendations

### 2. Memory Preservation
- **Fragmented Memories**: Photos scattered across devices
- **No Context**: Images without the stories and feelings
- **Forgotten Details**: Where was that café? What was special about it?
- **No Geographic Organization**: Can't see your journey mapped out

## The Solution

Viraha combines Letterboxd's curation philosophy with location-based discovery:

### Core Principles

1. **People Over Algorithms**
   - Discover through travelers you trust
   - No star ratings or numeric scores
   - Authentic storytelling over metrics

2. **Geography as Framework**
   - Every memory pinned to a location
   - Map-centric navigation
   - Spatial organization of experiences

3. **Balanced Content**
   - Location, text, and photos equally important
   - Not photo-heavy like Instagram
   - Not text-heavy like blogs
   - Just right

4. **Context-Aware Experience**
   - App adapts to where you are
   - Different needs at home vs traveling
   - Seamless mode switching

## How It Works

### The Dual-Mode System

**Local Mode** (When at home):
- Feed shows posts from people you follow
- Map displays only YOUR pins and memories
- Purpose: Share local knowledge, curate collection
- Social discovery through trusted network

**Traveling Mode** (When away from home):
- Feed shows posts from current geographic area
- Map displays EVERYONE'S pins nearby
- Purpose: Discover places, document journey
- Location-based community discovery

**Mode Switching**:
- Automatic GPS detection
- User confirmation required
- Manual override available
- Clear UI indicator

### Content Types

**Posts** - The atomic unit:
- Location tag (required)
- Caption/description (text-first)
- 1-4 photos (Twitter-style grid)
- Mode badge (optional)
- Public by default

**Albums** - Flexible containers:
- Collections of posts
- Journal entries (text memories)
- Scrapbook canvases (visual design - future)
- Privacy controls (public/private)
- Multi-location support

**Journal Entries**:
- Rich text editor
- Freeform writing
- Optional location
- Lives within albums

### Discovery Mechanism

**No Ratings, Just Curation**:
- Follow travelers whose taste aligns with yours
- Algorithm learns from your saves and interactions
- Quality through people, not metrics
- Avoids becoming Yelp/TripAdvisor

**Personalization Factors**:
- Who you follow (social graph)
- What you save (implicit interest)
- Places you visit (location patterns)
- Content you engage with (time spent)

## User Journeys

### Journey 1: The Local Curator
**Profile**: Sarah lives in Portland, loves finding hidden gems

**Flow**:
1. Discovers new café in her neighborhood
2. Opens Viraha → Create Post
3. Tags location, adds photos, writes about the vibe
4. Posts marked with "Local" badge
5. Her followers see it in their feed
6. Pin appears on her personal map

**Value**: Shares local knowledge, builds curated collection

### Journey 2: The Traveler
**Profile**: Marco is visiting Tokyo for the first time

**Flow**:
1. Phone detects he's far from home
2. Viraha: "Switch to Traveling Mode?"
3. Feed shows posts from Tokyo area
4. Map shows community pins nearby
5. Discovers café recommended by someone he follows
6. Visits and creates his own post
7. Adds post to "Japan" album

**Value**: Discovers authentic places, documents journey

### Journey 3: The Memory Keeper
**Profile**: Lisa returns from 3-week Europe trip

**Flow**:
1. Creates album "Summer in Europe"
2. Adds all posts from the trip
3. Writes journal entries for key moments
4. Sets album to private (personal memories)
5. Later, browses map to relive the journey
6. Shares specific album publicly when ready

**Value**: Preserves memories geographically, controls privacy

## Target Audience

### Primary Users

**Thoughtful Travelers** (Ages 25-45):
- Value authentic experiences over tourist traps
- Want to document travel meaningfully
- Tired of Instagram's superficiality
- Appreciate minimalist design

**Digital Nomads & Frequent Travelers**:
- Need practical discovery tools
- Want to preserve long-term travel
- Build community with like-minded travelers
- Share tips and recommendations

**Local Explorers**:
- Proud of their city
- Love discovering new places
- Want to share hidden gems
- Build local following

### Secondary Users

**Occasional Travelers**:
- 2-3 trips per year
- Want to remember trips years later
- Share with friends and family
- Less focused on discovery, more on preservation

**Travel Content Creators**:
- Share travel tips and guides
- Build following through authentic content
- Alternative to Instagram/YouTube
- Monetization potential (future)

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| App | Strength | Weakness | Our Difference |
|-----|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Instagram** | Visual storytelling, massive network | Algorithm chaos, metrics-driven, shallow | Minimalist, location-first, no likes |
| **Google Maps** | Comprehensive data, reviews | Transactional, impersonal, cluttered | Personal curation, storytelling |
| **TripAdvisor** | Reviews, bookings | Corporate, generic, ad-heavy | Community-driven, authentic |
| **Polarsteps** | Auto-tracking | Limited social features | Better social + manual curation |
| **AllTrails** | Outdoor-specific | Narrow use case | All types of travel |

### Our Positioning

**Viraha = Letterboxd for Travel**
- Letterboxd succeeded by focusing on curation over ratings
- We bring same philosophy to travel
- Collection > Consumption
- Quality > Quantity
- People > Algorithms

## Success Metrics

### North Star Metric
**Posts created per user per month** - Indicates engagement and value

### Key Metrics

**User Engagement**:
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Weekly Active Users (WAU)
- Mode switches per week
- Time in app per session

**Content Quality**:
- Average caption length (target: 100+ words)
- Photos per post (1-4 range)
- Albums created per user
- Save-to-album rate

**Social Growth**:
- New user signups
- User return rates
- Follow/follower ratio
- Profile visits

**Discovery**:
- Location searches
- Map interactions
- Feed scroll depth
- Traveling mode adoption

## Brand Identity

- **Thoughtful**: Not rushed or superficial
- **Authentic**: Real experiences, real places

- Subtle, earthy color palette

- Photography-friendly but not photo-dominant

### Taglines (Options)

- "The ache fades. The memories stay."
- "Curate your journey"
- "Where you've been. Where you're going."
